POEMS




my light

your light shines brightly on me
reflecting a comfy shelter
as your light brings joy to others
without even knowing so

are you there my firefly?
gloomy nights are spent without you
i’m sorry for being a knight
your safety matters to me

my sunshine, didn’t you know?
you are exquisite in my eyes
people say “the eyes never lie”
for it reflects on your soul

my firefly, forgive me
for being naïve and stubborn
as I close my eyes and whisper
i love you from the stars and back

flower

for you know who I’m referring to
ray of light that brightens my day
your simper is as bright as Sirius
a precious smile that’ll never fade

my dearest sunflower, my sunshine
in my sad days, you cheer me up
you bring color to my gloomy world
and you make sure everything’s fine

bloom vibrantly amidst the devoid
even when mass destruction hits you
you stood tall with your vibrant color
and I, have never felt this proud

so my everlasting ray of joy
you whose rays touch my fragile heart
beam your smile at me, don’t let it fade
because I, will always love your smile

stars

they twinkle earning the hearts of others
loved by many, delighting others
wished by a few for its true beauty
a light incomparable to others

i look in the sky and see it twinkle
gaining my utmost curiosity
and gaining my utmost attention
not knowing my heart is falling for her

they once said that we should not trust our hearts
for it falls too much, it falls too deep
but love risks, love thrives , love understands
most of all, it shelters another heart
